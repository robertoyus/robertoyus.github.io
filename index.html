Biography

I’m Roberto Yus, Postdoctoral Researcher at the University of California, Irvine working with Prof. Sharad Mehrotra in the ISG group. Previously I was a PhD student at the University of Zaragoza under Prof. Eduardo Mena and a visiting scholar at UMBC (working with Profs. Tim Finin and Anupam Joshi) and UCI. My research interests concern the Internet of Things, Privacy, Data Management, and Semantic Web. Specifically semantic and privacy preserving data management in IoT environments.
