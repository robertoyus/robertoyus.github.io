Students

I’m actively looking for students who are interested in data management, knowledge representation, privacy, and/or the Internet of Things. You can check out my projects and recent publications to figure out what I am doing that interests you the most. I’m especially looking for motivated Ph.D. students who want to work with me on the design of semantic data management solutions to empower IoT systems to understand user information requirements, as well as their privacy preferences, and tailor their operations to satisfy both. I’m also looking for highly motivated Master’s and Undergraduate students. Send me an email (ryus ‘at’ umbc.edu) and tell me about what I am doing that interests you!
